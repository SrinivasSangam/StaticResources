About LiquidHub

  1. Home
  2. About LiqudHub

LiquidHub is a customer engagement company that partners with businesses to improve customer experience and drive growth. Headquartered in Philadelphia, we serve companies globally, helping them solve their most complex challenges through design expertise and technology innovation. We bring success to our clients with a culture rooted in thought leadership and delivery excellence. Founded in 2001, LiquidHub believes in establishing strong bonds that have clear strategic direction and a shared commitment dedicated to fueling digital transformation and innovation. Our agile and responsive teams bring deep technology expertise and business-relevant solutions to every partnership.

When LiquidHub was formed, we considered the challenges our clients faced in integrating their enterprise technologies. They needed a way of tying it all together - what we thought of as a “hub”. Of course, such a hub is formed from a complex set of solutions and processes, always changing. A solution to this problem must be adaptable, must be "liquid."
